# Product

Scope: the **ปิ่น (Pin) marketing site** (`site/`). The Flutter app has its own
in-app design system (PinPalette/PinField/PinButton); this context governs the
web landing, not the app UI.

## Register

brand

## Users
Thai consumers choosing a personal AI assistant for their phone — office
workers, students, families, freelancers, and small-shop/SME owners. Context:
on mobile, mid-day, wanting help with small recurring tasks (reminders, drafts,
questions, errands). Many are quietly uneasy about AI apps harvesting their
data. The job to be done: *get a capable everyday assistant without handing a
company my private life.*

## Product Purpose
ปิ่น is an on-device, end-to-end-encrypted Thai AI assistant: the brain runs on
the phone, chats are E2EE, and the server is "blind" (sees only the minimum a
tool needs, never identity or conversation). The site's job is to convert a
visitor into a download by making the privacy promise *felt* (warm, not
fear-based), proving usefulness with concrete Thai use-cases, and being plainly
honest. Success = downloads + trust; the visitor should leave thinking "this one
is actually mine."

## Brand Personality
Three words: **อุ่นใจ · เป็นส่วนตัว · น่าเชื่อถือ** (warm · private · trustworthy).
Voice: gentle, human, Thai-native (the assistant speaks softly, ends with ค่ะ),
reassuring and honest — never hypey, never alarmist. Emotional goal: calm
confidence. The reader should feel *looked after*, and quietly certain their
data stays theirs.

## Anti-references
- **Crypto/Web3 hype** — no neon-on-black, aggressive gradients, countdowns, or
  "to the moon" urgency. Privacy here is care, not crypto-bro.
- **Generic SaaS-cream AI-landing template** — avoid the saturated default:
  cream bg + one accent + identical icon→heading→text card grids + a tiny
  tracked eyebrow above every section. (Pin *is* green/cream, so carry warmth in
  type, content, and the product mock — not by defaulting to the template shape.)
- **Cold enterprise/corporate** — no sterile navy-and-gray, stock boardroom
  photos, or B2B jargon. Pin is personal, not procurement.
- **Kiddie / cartoonish** — no mascot-heavy, bubbly, toy-like styling that would
  undercut the privacy/trust message.

## Design Principles
1. **Privacy as warmth, not paranoia.** Lead with "ส่วนตัวจริง ๆ", but the
   feeling is reassurance, not fear. Show the blind-proxy flow as a calm promise.
2. **Show real moments.** Concrete Thai use-cases + a real chat exchange beat an
   abstract feature list. Let the product mock do the talking.
3. **Honest, no hype.** No fake scarcity, no inflated claims. The privacy claim
   is literal and must stay literally true ("เซิร์ฟเวอร์มองไม่เห็น").
4. **Thai-native warmth.** Language, particles, and tone read local and human,
   not translated.
5. **Restraint over decoration.** One green accent, generous whitespace; earn
   attention with content and a believable product, not gradients or ornament.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG **AA**: body text ≥4.5:1 and large text ≥3:1 against its background;
visible keyboard focus; semantic landmarks/headings. **prefers-reduced-motion**
honored (reveal/tab transitions collapse to instant). Don't rely on color alone
(privacy/flow states also carry text + icon). Thai line-height kept generous for
legibility.
